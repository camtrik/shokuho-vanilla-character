Vanilla Character Suite & New Presets for Female Characters in Shokuho
========================================

English
-------
### Vanilla Characters
These override mods swap Shokuho’s face presets back to the vanilla Bannerlord versions while keeping Shokuho’s Japanese voice packs plus its hair/skin color restrictions.  
Four variants are included so you can pick exactly what you need:
- Vanilla Face – bannerlord face with Shokuho body shapes  
- Vanilla Character – bannerlord face and bannerlord body shapes
- Vanilla Face Female – same as vanilla Face, only affects female characters  
- Vanilla Character Female – same as Vanilla Character but only affects female characters  

Recommend one of the Shokuho-body variants, because many Shokuho armors rely on those body meshes; wearing them with vanilla body shapes can make parts of the torso disappear.

### New Presets for Female Characters in Shokuho
Create new presets for female characters in Shokuho.
[Here](https://github.com/camtrik/shokuho-vanilla-character/blob/main/character_presets.md) is the preset code.

中文

### 原版角色
将织丰中的脸模替换为原版脸模，同时保留了织丰中对发色，肤色的限制以及日语语音。
有四种变体可以选择：
- 原本脸模+织丰身形 - Vanilla Face
- 原版脸模+原版身形 - Vanilla Character
- 原本脸模+织丰身形（只对女性生效）- Vanilla Face Female
- 原版脸模+原版身形（只对女性生效）- Vanilla Character Female 

推荐使用带织丰身形的版本（由于织丰中很多装备已经和织丰的身形绑定，原版身形穿织丰装备可能出现身体部分变透明的问题）

### 织丰女性新的捏脸预设
给织丰中的女性角色准备了新的捏脸预设。
预设中用到的代码在[这里](https://github.com/camtrik/shokuho-vanilla-character/blob/main/character_presets.md)
